# 🛡️ VidyaCore: MDX Article Norms & Standards

This document establishes the official "Shastric Standard" for all long-form content in the VidyaCore ecosystem.

---

## 🕉️ 1. The Opening Mantra (Rule of Authenticity)
Every article must begin with a foundational verse that serves as the "Bija" (Seed) of the content.

### Formatting Requirements:
1.  **Devanagari**: The original script for spiritual potency.
2.  **IAST**: The academic transliteration.
3.  **Source Link**: A direct hyperlink to the verified verse on [Vedabase.io](https://vedabase.io).

---

## 🎨 2. Visual & Aesthetic Standards
To create a premium, meditative experience:
- **Asset Location**: All images must be stored in `client/src/assets/images/`.
- **Naming Convention**: `[node-slug]-aesthetic.webp`.
- **Design Motif**: Use subtle shastric elements (Peacock feathers, lotuses, flutes) to frame the knowledge.

---

## 🔗 3. External Resource Linking
- **Primary Source**: Always link to [Vedabase.io](https://vedabase.io) for Bhagavad Gita and Bhagavata Purana verses.
- **Structure**: `[Verse Reference](https://vedabase.io/[book]/[c]/[v])`.

---

## 🧬 4. MDX Frontmatter
Every file must contain the following metadata:
```yaml
---
title: "Article Headline"
slug: "clean-url-slug"
node_id: [ID from Kalpataru Table]
node_type: "MULA | VYAKHYA | etc."
description: "A 1-2 sentence summary for SEO."
featured_image: "/assets/images/..."
---
```
