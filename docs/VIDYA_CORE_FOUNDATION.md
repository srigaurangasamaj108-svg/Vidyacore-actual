# VidyaCore: Foundational Vision 🌿📖

VidyaCore is a unified digital ecosystem designed for the preservation, study, and dissemination of Vedic knowledge.

---

## 🌟 1. The Vision
To build a system where **Shastra remains pure**, **meaning expands freely**, and **knowledge is interconnected** across traditions and centuries.

---

## ⚖️ 2. The "Dharma" (Core Design Rules)

1.  **Separation of Layers**: Structure, Canon, and Interpretation must never be mixed.
2.  **Mula Purity**: The original text (`mula`) must be stored exactly as originated, without translation or commentary.
3.  **Vyakhya Freedom**: The system must allow for infinite, coexisting interpretations from different traditions.
4.  **Taxonomy Discipline**: All conceptual classification must follow a curated, hierarchical ontology (no free tagging).
5.  **Explicit Relationships**: Every connection between texts or concepts must be modeled and justified.
6.  **Graph Readiness**: The schema is designed such that every row can be mapped to a node in a knowledge graph (e.g., Neo4j).
7.  **Identity-First Authority**: Every contribution must be linked to a verifiable Authority (Person) and Lineage (Sampradaya).

---

## 🧠 3. The 3 Pillars of VidyaCore

### I. Integrity (Satya)
Ensured through PostgreSQL constraints, type-safe code, and strict validation. The knowledge tree cannot be corrupted by "loose" data.

### II. Connectivity (Sambandha)
Every verse is linked to its root, its commentators, its parallel teachings, and its conceptual themes. Knowledge is a living web, not an isolated list.

### III. Discovery (Bodha)
Using AI-powered semantic search, the system allows users to discover ancient wisdom through modern language, finding meaning where keywords fail.

---

## 🙏 4. The Goal
VidyaCore is built not just for today's scholars, but for **future generations**. It is a scalable, resilient foundation for the digital Vedic Renaissance.

> "Preserve truth. Enable understanding. Prevent distortion."
