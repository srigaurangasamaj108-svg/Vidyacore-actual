## **The Vedas (वेदाः): Eternal Wisdom Manifested**

The **Vedas** are the foundational scriptures of Sanātana Dharma, revealed directly by the Supreme Lord at the beginning of creation. They are **apauruṣeya** (not of human origin) and contain the totality of knowledge required for both material prosperity and spiritual liberation.

### **The Fourfold Division**

The single Veda was divided into four by Sage Vyāsadeva to make it accessible in the age of Kali:

1. **Ṛg Veda** — The Veda of Hymns and Mantras.
2. **Yajur Veda** — The Veda of Ritual and Sacrifice.
3. **Sāma Veda** — The Veda of Song and Melody.
4. **Atharva Veda** — The Veda of Daily Life and Protection.
