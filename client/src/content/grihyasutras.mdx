## **Gṛhyasūtras (गृह्यसूत्राणि): The Sanctification of Life**

The **Gṛhyasūtras** are the guides for the household spiritual life. They focus on the rituals that a householder (**Gṛhastha**) performs in their single domestic fire, marking the significant transitions and daily duties of human existence.

### **Focus & Key Texts**

* **Rituals**: The 16 Saṃskāras (Conception, Naming, Marriage, Funerals, etc.).
* **Key Texts**: Āpastamba, Pāraskara, and Gobhila Gṛhyasūtras.
