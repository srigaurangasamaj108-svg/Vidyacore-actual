## **Vedāṅga (वेदाङ्ग): The Disciplines of Understanding**

To correctly interpret and utilize the vast wisdom of the Vedas, six auxiliary disciplines known as **Vedāṅgas** were established. These are the "limbs" of the Veda, each serving a specific epistemic function.

### **The Six Vedāṅgas**

1. **Śikṣā** (Phonetics) — The science of proper articulation and pronunciation.
2. **Kalpa** (Ritual) — The rules for sacrificial procedures and conduct.
3. **Vyākaraṇa** (Grammar) — The linguistic structure of the sacred sound.
4. **Nirukta** (Etymology) — The derivation and meaning of Vedic words.
5. **Chandas** (Prosody) — The science of poetic meters and rhythms.
6. **Jyotiṣa** (Astronomy) — The science of time and planetary alignment.

> "Without the limbs, the body of the Veda remains inactive in the seeker's life."
