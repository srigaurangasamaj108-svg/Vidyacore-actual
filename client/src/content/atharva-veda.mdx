## **Atharva Veda (अथर्ववेदः): Wisdom for Living**

The **Atharva Veda** is distinct from the other three Vedas in its focus on the practical and mundane aspects of human existence. It contains mantras for healing, protection against negative influences, and the prosperity of society and the household.

### **Key Characteristics**

* **Primary Content**: Spells, prayers, and medicinal observations.
* **Focus**: The application of spiritual power for health, longevity, and social order.
* **Priest**: Associated with the **Brahman** priest, who oversees the entire sacrifice.
