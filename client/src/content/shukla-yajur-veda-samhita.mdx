## **Śukla Yajur Veda Saṃhitā (शुक्ल यजुर्वेद संहिता): The Pure Sound**

The **Śukla Yajur Veda Saṃhitā** (primarily the **Vājasaneyi Saṃhitā**) is the collection of liturgical mantras used by the Adhvaryu priests. It is distinguished by its "pure" or "white" nature, where the mantras are maintained in a state of absolute separation from any ritual explanation or prose commentary.

### **Structure**

* **40 Adhyāyas** (Chapters).
* **Focus**: The invocation of cosmic order through precise ritual sound.

---

> "The sound of the White Yajur Veda is the breath of the Sun."
