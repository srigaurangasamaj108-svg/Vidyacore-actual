## **Kāraka (कारकम्): The Mechanism of Action**
In the Vyākaraṇa system, a sentence is not a string of words but an "action." The Kāraka system identifies the various roles participants play in this action: the agent (**Kartā**), the object (**Karma**), the instrument (**Karaṇa**), and more.

## **Sphoṭa (स्फोटः): Linguistic Revelation**
Argued by Bhartṛhari, Sphoṭa is the "bursting forth" of meaning. It suggests that language is a manifestation of consciousness itself—**Śabda Brahman**.
