## **Gāndharvaveda (गान्धर्ववेद): The Celestial Arts**

**Gāndharvaveda** is the science of sound and movement. It reveals how the arts—music, dance, and drama—can be used to harmonize the individual soul with the cosmic rhythm of the universe.

### **Key Aspects**

* **Rāga** — The melodic framework of sacred sound.
* **Tāla** — The rhythmic cycles that mirror the pulse of creation.
* **Rasa** — The aesthetic experience that leads to spiritual ecstasy.
