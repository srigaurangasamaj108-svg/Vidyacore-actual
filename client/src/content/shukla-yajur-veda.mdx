## **Śukla Yajur Veda (शुक्ल यजुर्वेदः): The Pure Revelation**

The **Śukla Yajur Veda** is known as the "White" Yajur Veda because of its clarity and organization. In this tradition, the sacred mantras (**Saṃhitā**) are maintained in a state of separation from the prose explanations and ritual instructions (**Brāhmaṇa**).

### **Foundational Characteristics**

* **Lineage**: Revealed to Sage Yājñavalkya by the Sun God (**Sūrya**).
* **Major Text**: The **Vājasaneyi Saṃhitā**.
* **Clarity**: The separation of mantra and commentary allows for a more focused liturgical experience.
