## **Ṛg Veda Brāhmaṇa (ऋग्वेद ब्राह्मणम्): The Science of Ritual**

The **Brāhmaṇa** portion of the Ṛg Veda provides the necessary instructions for the use of the hymns in sacrificial ceremonies. It explains the "why" and "how" of the Vedic ritual, often using symbolic narratives to reveal the deeper spiritual significance of the actions.

### **Primary Texts**

1. **Aitareya Brāhmaṇa** — Focusing on the duties of the Hotṛ priest.
2. **Kauṣītaki Brāhmaṇa** (or Śāṅkhāyana) — Providing alternative ritual perspectives.

---
