## **Atharva Veda Brāhmaṇa (अथर्ववेद ब्राह्मणम्): The Gopatha**

The primary Brāhmaṇa associated with the Atharva Veda is the **Gopatha Brāhmaṇa**. It provides the necessary ritual instructions and symbolic explanations for the mantras of the Atharva Veda, focusing on the role of the **Brahman** priest who oversees the entire sacrifice.

### **Focus Areas**

* **Ritual Purity**: The importance of protecting the integrity of the sacrifice.
* **Symbolism**: Elaborate narratives explaining the origins and power of the Atharva mantras.

---

> "The Brahman priest is the silent witness who ensures the success of the sacrifice."
