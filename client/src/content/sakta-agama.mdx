## **Śākta Āgama (शाक्तागम): The Path of Divine Power**

The **Śākta Āgamas** (often called Tantras) focus on the worship of the Supreme Energy (**Śakti**) as the dynamic manifestation of the Absolute. They emphasize the balance of spiritual knowledge with the mastery of universal energies.

### **The Foundations of Śāktism**

* **Mantraśāstra** — The science of sacred sound and vibration.
* **Yantraśāstra** — The science of geometric manifestation and concentration.
