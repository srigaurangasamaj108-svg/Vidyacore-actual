## **Vyavahāra & Rājadharma**

* **Aṣṭādaśa-vyavahāra-padāni (ID #294)**: The eighteen formal titles of law, including **Ṛṇa-dāna** (Debt), **Vibhāga** (Inheritance), and **Sāhasa** (Violence).
* **Rājadharma-śāstram (ID #268)**: The science of governance (**Daṇḍanīti**), where the King is manifest as the protector of the law and the maintainer of social harmony.

---

## **Purification & Restoration**

* **Prāyaścitta-vidhiḥ (ID #266)**: The science of expiation—how to restore equilibrium after a transgression through penance (**Tapas**) and purification (**Cāndrāyaṇa**).
