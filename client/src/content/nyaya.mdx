## **Nyāya (न्यायः): The Pillars of Reason**

**Nyāya** is the science of logical reasoning. It establishes the methods by which truth can be identified and distinguishes between valid knowledge and illusion.

### **The Four Pramāṇas (Sources of Knowledge)**

1. **Pratyakṣa** (Perception) — Direct observation through the senses.
2. **Anumāna** (Inference) — Reaching a conclusion through logical deduction.
3. **Upamāna** (Comparison) — Understanding through analogy and similarity.
4. **Śabda** (Testimony) — Knowledge received from authoritative sources (the Veda).
