## **Vijñānalalita (विज्ञानललित): The Internal Journey**

**Vijñānalalita** represents the more esoteric and contemplative stream of the Vaiṣṇava Āgamas. It emphasizes internal realization (**Vijñāna**) and the ecstatic bliss (**Lalita**) that arises from the soul's union with the Divine.

### **Key Themes**

* **Vijñāna** — Direct, experiential knowledge of the Supreme.
* **Bhāva** — The cultivation of deep spiritual emotion.
* **Internal Ritual** — The performance of worship within the sacred space of the heart.
