## **Kṛṣṇa Yajur Veda Āraṇyaka (कृष्ण यजुर्वेद आरण्यकम्): The Inner Path**

The **Taittirīya Āraṇyaka** contains the meditations and symbolic interpretations required for the seeker to internalize the Vedic sacrifice. It contains the famous "Sūrya Namaskāra" and other profound contemplative practices.
