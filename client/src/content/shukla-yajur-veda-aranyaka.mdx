## **Śukla Yajur Veda Āraṇyaka (शुक्ल यजुर्वेद आरण्यकम्): The Inner Sacrifice**

The Āraṇyaka of the Śukla Yajur Veda is the beginning portion of the **Bṛhadāraṇyaka** ("The Great Forest Treatise"). It marks the transition from the external rituals of the Śatapatha Brāhmaṇa to the pure internal contemplation of the self.
