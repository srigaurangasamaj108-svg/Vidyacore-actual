## **Atharva Veda Upaniṣad (अथर्ववेद उपनिषद्): The Final Realization**

The Upaniṣads of the Atharva Veda are among the most philosophically dense and influential in all of Vedānta:

1. **Muṇḍaka Upaniṣad** — Famous for the Mahāvākya: **Satyameva Jayate** ("Truth alone triumphs").
2. **Māṇḍūkya Upaniṣad** — Focusing on the four states of consciousness and the syllable **OM**.
3. **Praśna Upaniṣad** — Six questions concerning the nature of life and the soul.

---

> "Brahman is the bow; the self is the arrow; and the Absolute is the target." — **Muṇḍaka Upaniṣad 2.2.4**
