## **Samayāgama (समयागम): The Path of Sublimation**

**Samayāgama** (associated with the "Right-hand path" or **Dakṣiṇācāra**) represents the most refined and internal expression of Śakti worship. In this path, external rituals are replaced by symbolic meditations within the consciousness of the seeker.

### **Foundational Principles**

* **Ontology**: Purely Sattvic approach where all manifestation is seen as a movement of consciousness (**Vimarśa**).
* **Practice**: Meditation on the **Śrīcakra**, pure mantra japa, and contemplative absorption in the union of Śiva and Śakti.
* **Goal**: The sublimation of all energies into the pure light of awareness.
