## **Yajur Veda (यजुर्वेदः): The Technology of Sacrifice**

The **Yajur Veda** serves as the practical manual for the performance of Vedic sacrifices (**Yajñas**). It contains the prose mantras (**Yajus**) that guide the physical actions and offerings of the ritual process.

### **The Two Major Recensions**

1. **Kṛṣṇa Yajur Veda** (Black) — Where the mantras are intermingled with prose explanations.
2. **Śukla Yajur Veda** (White) — Where the mantras are clearly separated from the explanatory Brahmana text.

---

*   **Focus**: Ritual precision and cosmic alignment.
*   **Priest**: Associated with the **Adhvaryu** priest.
