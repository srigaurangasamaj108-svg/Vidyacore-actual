## **Trimuni (त्रिमुनि): The Three Pillars**

The **Trimuni** tradition forms the absolute authority of the Vyākaraṇa Vedāṅga. It consists of the lineage of three Sages who progressively refined the science of Sanskrit:

1. **Pāṇini (Sūtrakāra)**: The author of the Aṣṭādhyāyī, the core rules of grammar.
2. **Kātyāyana (Vārttikakāra)**: The author of the Vārttikas, which provide critical additions and refinements to Pāṇini's rules.
3. **Patañjali (Bhāṣyakāra)**: The author of the Mahābhāṣya, the definitive commentary that explains and defends the entire system.
