## **The Metaphysics of Action**

* **Śabda-darśanam (ID #186)**: The philosophy that words and their meanings are eternally connected, and the Veda is the manifestation of eternal sound.
* **Padārtha-vicāraḥ (ID #190)**: The ontological study of **Dharma** (merit), **Adharma**, and the invisible causal force (**Apūrva**) that links a finite act to an infinite result.
