## **Pratishṭhāsāra (प्रतिष्ठासार): Manifesting the Divine**

**Pratishṭhāsāra** centers on the vital rituals of **Pratiṣṭhā**—the consecration of temples and the installation of sacred icons (**Arcā-vigrahas**). It provides the exact procedures for grounding divine energy in the manifest world.

### **Focus Areas**

* **Pratiṣṭhā** — The complex rituals that transform a physical icon into a manifest form of the Lord.
* **Maṇḍapa Science** — The construction of sacred spaces for rituals.
* **Pratyakṣa** — The process of making the Divine visible to the seeker.
