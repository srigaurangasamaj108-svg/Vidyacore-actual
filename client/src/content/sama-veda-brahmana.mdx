## **Sāma Veda Brāhmaṇa (सामवेद ब्राह्मणम्): The Science of Song**

The **Brāhmaṇa** portion of the Sāma Veda provides the detailed instructions for the use of the melodic chants in the Soma sacrifices. It explains the relationship between the sound, the ritual action, and the desired cosmic result.

### **Primary Texts**

The Sāma Veda is known for having a large number of Brāhmaṇas (traditionally eight), including:

* **Pañcaviṃśa Brāhmaṇa** — One of the oldest and most important.
* **Ṣaḍviṃśa Brāhmaṇa** — Focusing on various sacrificial procedures.
* **Jaiminīya Brāhmaṇa** — Rich in philosophical and symbolic narratives.
