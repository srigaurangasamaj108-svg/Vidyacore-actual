## **Śikṣā (शिक्षा): The Nose of the Veda**

**Śikṣā** is the science of phonetics and phonology. It ensures that the sacred mantras of the Vedas are pronounced with absolute precision, as the power of the Veda resides in its sound (**Sabda**).

### **Key Aspects**

* **Varṇa** (Letters)
* **Svara** (Accents)
* **Mātrā** (Duration)
* **Bala** (Strength of articulation)
* **Sāma** (Melody)
* **Santāna** (Continuity)

---

> "Proper pronunciation is the first step in the manifestation of Vedic power."
