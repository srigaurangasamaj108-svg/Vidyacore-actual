## **Ṛg Veda Upaniṣad (ऋग्वेद उपनिषद्): The Realization of Oneness**

The **Upaniṣads** of the Ṛg Veda represent the culmination of Vedic wisdom. They move beyond the ritual and the symbol to directly address the nature of ultimate reality (**Brahman**) and the eternal self (**Ātman**).

### **Primary Texts**

* **Aitareya Upaniṣad** — Famous for the Mahāvākya: **Prajñānaṃ Brahma** ("Consciousness is Brahman").
* **Kauṣītaki Upaniṣad** — Exploring the journey of the soul and the nature of the life-force.
