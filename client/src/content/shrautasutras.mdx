## **Śrautasūtras (श्रौतसूत्राणि): The Macrocosmic Sacrifice**

The **Śrautasūtras** are the foundational manuals for the performance of the major Vedic sacrifices. They focus on rituals that are not performed by the householder alone but require a group of specialized priests and the establishment of three or more sacred fires.

### **Focus & Key Texts**

* **Rituals**: Soma sacrifices, Aśvamedha, Rājasūya, and Agnicayana.
* **Key Texts**: Baudhāyana, Āśvalāyana, and Kātyāyana Śrautasūtras.
