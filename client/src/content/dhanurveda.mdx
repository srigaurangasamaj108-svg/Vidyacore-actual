## **Dhanurveda (धनुर्वेद): The Path of the Warrior**

**Dhanurveda** is the science of defense. It provides the knowledge required to protect society and maintain dharma through the mastery of weapons and strategic combat.

### **Key Aspects**

* **Astra** — Weapons guided by mantras and spiritual power.
* **Śastra** — Physical weapons and hand-to-hand combat.
* **Yuddha-dharma** — The ethical codes that govern righteous warfare.
