## **Pāṇinīya Śikṣā (पाणिनीयशिक्षा)**
The definitive guide to Sanskrit pronunciation, detailing the 63/64 sounds and the five factors of articulation: sthāna (place), prayatna (effort), kāla (time), svara (accent), and anupradāna (breath).

## **Nāradīya Śikṣā (नारदीयशिक्षा)**
Unique for its focus on the relationship between Vedic accents and musical notes (Svaras), particularly for the Sāmaveda.
