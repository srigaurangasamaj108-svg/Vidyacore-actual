## **Ṛg Veda (ऋग्वेदः): The Sacred Sound of Praise**

The **Ṛg Veda** is the most ancient layer of Vedic literature. It consists of 1,028 hymns (**Sūktas**) organized into ten books known as **Maṇḍalas**. These hymns are primarily poetic expressions of praise and invocation directed toward the Supreme and His various cosmic manifestations.

### **Key Characteristics**

* **Primary Content**: Mantras (**Ṛks**) intended for recitation.
* **Focus**: The realization of divine qualities through sound.
* **Priest**: Associated with the **Hotṛ** priest.
