## **Rudra-bheda (रुद्रभेद): The Path of Integration**

The **Rudra-bheda** consists of eighteen Āgamas that move beyond strict dualism into a "qualified non-dualism" (**Bhedābheda**). These texts begin to bridge the gap between the Lord's transcendence and His immanence within the soul.

### **Foundational Principles**

* **Ontology**: Śiva is both beyond the world and present within the consciousness of the soul.
* **Practice**: Greater emphasis on **Yoga** and internal realization alongside external ritual.
* **Key Āgamas**: Raurava, Mṛgendra, Vijaya, and Svāyambhuva.
