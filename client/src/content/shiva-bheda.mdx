## **Śiva-bheda (शिवभेद): The Path of Distinction**

The **Śiva-bheda** consists of ten Āgamas revealed from the five faces of Lord Śiva. These texts emphasize a dualistic ontology (**Bheda**), maintaining a clear distinction between the Lord (**Pati**), the soul (**Paśu**), and the bondage (**Pāśa**).

### **Foundational Principles**

* **Ontology**: Śiva is completely transcendent and independent.
* **Ritual**: Focuses on temple construction, deity installation, and strict ritual purity.
* **Key Āgamas**: Kāmika, Yogaja, Chintya, Kāraṇa, and Ajita.
