## **Jyotiṣa (ज्योतिषम्): The Eyes of the Veda**

**Jyotiṣa** is the science of time (**Kāla**). it allows the seeker to align their actions with the cosmic cycles of the universe, ensuring that rituals and duties are performed at the most auspicious moments.

### **The Three Pillars of Jyotiṣa**

1. **Siddhānta** (Astronomy) — Mathematical calculation of planetary positions.
2. **Saṃhitā** (Cosmic Omens) — The study of celestial phenomena and their influence.
3. **Horā** (Astrology) — The study of time and its impact on the individual.
