## **Śaiva Āgama (शैवागम): The Path of Realization**

The **Śaiva Āgamas** reveal the knowledge and practice required to realize the identity with the Absolute through the grace of Lord Śiva. They cover the four aspects of knowledge (**vidyā**), action (**kriyā**), meditation (**yoga**), and conduct (**caryā**).

### **The Foundations of Śaivism**

* **Kāmika Āgama** — The primary among the 28 Mūla Āgamas of the Śaiva Siddhānta.
* **Kāraṇa Āgama** — Focusing on the causes of the universe and ritual procedures.
