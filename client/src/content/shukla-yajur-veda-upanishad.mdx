## **Śukla Yajur Veda Upaniṣad (शुक्ल यजुर्वेद उपनिषद्): The Final Truth**

The Upaniṣads of the Śukla Yajur Veda are among the most revered in the Vedic tradition:

1. **Bṛhadāraṇyaka Upaniṣad** — The largest and most profound, featuring the realizations of Sage Yājñavalkya.
2. **Īśa Upaniṣad** — The only Upaniṣad that is actually part of the Saṃhitā itself, emphasizing the omnipresence of the Divine.

---

> "From the unreal lead me to the Real; from darkness lead me to Light; from death lead me to Immortality."
