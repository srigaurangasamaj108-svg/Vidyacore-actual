## **Mīmāṃsā: The Science of Inquiry**

Mīmāṃsā is the logical engine that unlocks the Veda. It is traditionally divided into two great streams:

1. **Pūrva Mīmāṃsā (ID #182)**: Focused on **Dharma** and **Karma-kāṇḍa** (Ritual Action). It utilizes Sage Jaimini's Sūtras to define how we should act.
2. **Uttara Mīmāṃsā (ID #183)**: Known as **Vedānta**, focused on **Brahman** and **Jñāna-kāṇḍa** (Knowledge). It utilizes Sage Bādarāyaṇa's Brahma Sūtras to define who we are.

## **The Foundation of Knowledge**

Uttara Mīmāṃsā stands upon the **Prasthānatrayī** (The Triple Foundation):
* **The Upaniṣads** (Śruti-prasthāna)
* **The Bhagavad Gītā** (Smṛti-prasthāna)
* **The Brahma Sūtras** (Nyāya-prasthāna)
