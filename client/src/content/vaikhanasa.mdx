## **Vaikhānasa (वैखानस): The Sage's Legacy**

The **Vaikhānasa** tradition is uniquely positioned as a bridge between the Vedic and Agamic streams. It trace its origin to Sage Vikhanas and emphasizes the performance of rituals in strict accordance with Vedic mantras and principles.

### **Foundational Characteristics**

* **Origin**: Revealed by Lord Nārāyaṇa to Sage Vikhanas.
* **Focus**: Temple rituals (**Arcanā**) performed as a form of sacrifice (**Yajña**).
* **Lineage**: Continued through the disciples Bhrigu, Marichi, Atri, and Kashyapa.
