## **Pāṇini (पाणिनिः): The Sūtrakāra**
The core authority of the system. His Aṣṭādhyāyī is a marvel of mathematical and linguistic engineering.

## **Kātyāyana (कात्यायनः): The Vārttikakāra**
His Vārttikas refine and update the rules of Pāṇini to ensure their continued accuracy as the language evolved.

The author of the Mahābhāṣya, providing the ultimate synthesis and logical defense of the Paninian system.
