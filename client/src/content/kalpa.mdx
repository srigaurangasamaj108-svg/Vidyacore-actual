## **Kalpa (कल्प): The Hands of the Veda**

**Kalpa** provides the systematic rules for the performance of Vedic rituals and the conduct of daily life. It is the practical application of Vedic theory to human action.

### **The Four Śulba Sūtras**

1. **Śrauta Sūtras** — Rules for major public sacrifices.
2. **Gṛhya Sūtras** — Rules for household rituals and life-cycle events (**Saṃskāras**).
3. **Dharma Sūtras** — Rules for social, moral, and legal conduct.
4. **Śulba Sūtras** — The science of altar construction and sacred geometry.
