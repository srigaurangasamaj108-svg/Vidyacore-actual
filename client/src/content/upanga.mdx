## **Upāṅga (उपाङ्ग): The Pillars of Logic and Law**

The **Upāṅgas** provide the intellectual framework for understanding the Veda's philosophical conclusions. They establish the laws of logic, the history of dharma, and the codes of social and moral conduct.

### **The Primary Upāṅgas**

* **Purāṇa** — The historical records that explain the Vedas through narratives.
* **Nyāya** — The science of logic and epistemology.
* **Mīmāṃsā** — The philosophy of scriptural interpretation.
* **Dharmaśāstra** — The codes of moral and civil law (e.g., Manu-smṛti).
