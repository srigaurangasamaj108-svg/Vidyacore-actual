## **Ṛg Veda Āraṇyaka (ऋग्वेद आरण्यकम्): The Internal Altar**

The **Āraṇyakas** serve as a bridge between the ritualistic Brāhmaṇas and the philosophical Upaniṣads. They were traditionally studied by hermits and forest-dwellers who replaced external sacrifices with internal meditation and symbolic contemplation.

### **Primary Texts**

* **Aitareya Āraṇyaka** — Containing deep meditations on the nature of life and sound.
* **Kauṣītaki Āraṇyaka** — Focusing on the prāṇa (life-breath) as the ultimate offering.
