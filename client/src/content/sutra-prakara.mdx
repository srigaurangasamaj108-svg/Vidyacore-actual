## **Sūtra-Prakāra (सूत्रप्रकाराः): The Logic of Rule**

To navigate the Aṣṭādhyāyī, one must understand the six types of sūtras (**Ṣadvidhaṃ Sūtram**):

1. **Saṃjñā**: Definitions (e.g., 'Guṇa').
2. **Paribhāṣā**: Meta-rules (How to interpret rules).
3. **Vidhi**: Operational rules (Rules that perform an action).
4. **Niyama**: Restrictive rules (Limiting the scope of a rule).
5. **Atideśa**: Extension rules (Applying qualities by analogy).
6. **Adhikāra**: Header rules (Defining the context of subsequent rules).
