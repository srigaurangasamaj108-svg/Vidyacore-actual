## **Dharma-mūlam: The Root of Law**

Dharmaśāstra is the application of Vedic truth into social reality. It is anchored by the **Dharmamūla** (The four sources of Dharma):
1. **Śruti** (Veda)
2. **Smṛti** (Tradition)
3. **Sadācāra** (Conduct of the righteous)
4. **Ātmatuṣṭi** (Inner conscience)

## **Literary Foundation**

* **Smṛti-granthaḥ (ID #263)**: Foundational social laws like the **Manu Smṛti**, **Yājñavalkya Smṛti**, and the Kali-yuga authority, **Parāśara Smṛti**.
* **Nibandhas**: regional legal digests like the **Mitākṣarā** and **Dayabhāga** that harmonized the ancient codes for practical governance.
