## **Bhairava Tantra (भैरवतन्त्र): Absolute Non-Dualism**

**Bhairava Tantra** (associated with **Kashmir Shaivism** or **Trika**) represents the culmination of Śaiva philosophy. It emphasizes absolute non-dualism (**Abheda**), where the individual self is recognized as being fundamentally identical to Lord Śiva.

### **Foundational Principles**

* **Ontology**: Recognition (**Pratyabhijñā**) that the self is already Śiva; there is no real bondage, only ignorance.
* **Practice**: Ritual is internalized; emphasis on spontaneous recognition and the mastery of consciousness.
* **Key Texts**: Tantrāloka, Mālinīvijaya Tantra, and Svacchanda Tantra.
