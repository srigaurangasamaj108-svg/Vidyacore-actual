## **Āyurveda (आयुर्वेद): The Wisdom of Longevity**

**Āyurveda** is the science of life. It provides the knowledge required to maintain a healthy body and mind, allowing the seeker to fulfill their dharma without the obstacle of disease.

### **Foundational Principles**

* **Tridoṣa** — The three cosmic energies (**Vāyu**, **Pitta**, **Kapha**) that govern the physical body.
* **Pañcamahābhūta** — The five great elements that compose all manifest matter.
* **Health** — Defined as the equilibrium of the doṣas and the clarity of the soul.
