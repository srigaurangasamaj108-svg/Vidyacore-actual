## **Sūtra-Prakāra: The Functional Types**

* **Saṃjñā**: Technical definitions.
* **Paribhāṣā**: Meta-rules of interpretation.
* **Vidhi**: Actionable rules of sound change.
* **Niyama**: Restrictive boundaries.
* **Atideśa**: Analogical extensions.
* **Adhikāra**: Contextual headers.
