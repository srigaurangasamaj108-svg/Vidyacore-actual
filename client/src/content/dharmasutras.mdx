## **Dharmasūtras (धर्मसूत्राणि): The Foundation of Harmony**

The **Dharmasūtras** represent the transition from ritual action to ethical and social conduct. They provide the laws and customs that sustain the individual and society, serving as the direct precursors to the later Dharma Śāstras.

### **Focus & Key Texts**

* **Focus**: Varṇa Dharma (Social classes) and Āśrama Dharma (Stages of life).
* **Key Texts**: Gautama, Baudhāyana, and Āpastamba Dharmasūtras.

---

> "Dharma is that which sustains the individual and the universe in perfect harmony."
