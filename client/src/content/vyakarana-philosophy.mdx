## **Philosophy & Process (दर्शनं प्रक्रिया च): The Deep Structure**

Vyākaraṇa is more than a technical science; it is a **Darśana** (philosophical school). It explores how meaning is manifest in consciousness through speech:

* **Kāraka**: The syntactic relationship between words and actions.
* **Sphoṭa**: The theory of the "linguistic flash"—the instantaneous recognition of meaning.
* **Prakriyā**: The step-by-step derivation process that reveals the source of every word.
