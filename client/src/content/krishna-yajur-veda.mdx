## **Kṛṣṇa Yajur Veda (कृष्ण यजुर्वेदः): The Integrated Revelation**

The **Kṛṣṇa Yajur Veda** is known as the "Black" Yajur Veda because the mantras (**Saṃhitā**) and the explanatory ritual instructions (**Brāhmaṇa**) are intermingled within the text. This provides a more unified, though less "clear," structure for the practitioner.

### **Foundational Characteristics**

* **Lineage**: Associated with the sage Tittiri and the **Taittirīya** tradition.
* **Focus**: The deep integration of action and explanation.
* **Major Text**: The **Taittirīya Saṃhitā**.
