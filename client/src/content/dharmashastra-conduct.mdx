## **Ācāra-vicāraḥ: Righteous Living**

* **Ṣoḍaśa Saṃskāras (ID #278)**: The sixteen life-cycle rituals that transform a biological individual into a cultured being (**Dvija**), from conception (**Garbhādhāna**) to final rites (**Antyeṣṭi**).
* **Varṇāśrama-vyavasthā (ID #267)**: The dual map of social role (**Varṇa**) and life-stage (**Āśrama**) that maintains cosmic harmony (**Ṛta**) through localized duty.
