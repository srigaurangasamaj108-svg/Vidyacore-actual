## **Māheśvara Sūtras (शिवसूत्राणि)**
These sutras organize the sounds of Sanskrit into a highly structured grid that allows Pāṇini to define groups of phonemes with a single syllable.

## **Dhātupāṭha (धातुपाठः)**
Listing approximately 2,000 roots, this text explains how nouns and verbs are derived from active processes.
