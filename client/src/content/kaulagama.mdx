## **Kaulāgama (कौलागम): The Path of Breaking**

**Kaulāgama** (associated with the "Left-hand path" or **Vāmācāra**) represents the most radical expression of Śākta non-dualism. It operates on the principle that *nothing is outside the Divine*, using even taboo or mundane elements as tools for transcendence.

### **Foundational Principles**

* **Ontology**: Absolute non-dualism where the body and the world are seen as the manifest form of Śakti.
* **Practice**: Emphasizes the awakening of **Kuṇḍalinī** through powerful and often transgressive rituals.
* **Goal**: To break through social and mental constructs to realize the primal state of consciousness.
