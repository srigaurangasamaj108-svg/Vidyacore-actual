## **Nirukta (निरुक्तम्): The Ears of the Veda**

**Nirukta** is the science of scriptural interpretation through etymology. It reveals the roots of Vedic words, allowing the seeker to understand the deep, ontological meaning behind the mantras.

### **The Work of Yāska**

The primary text is **Yāska's Nirukta**, which provides the semantic bridge between the raw Vedic sound and its philosophical significance.

* **Nāma** (Nouns)
* **Ākhyāta** (Verbs)
* **Upasarga** (Prefixes)
* **Nipāta** (Particles)
