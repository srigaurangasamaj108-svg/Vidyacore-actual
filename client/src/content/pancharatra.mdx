## **Pancharātra (पाञ्चरात्र): The Doctrine of Five Nights**

**Pancharātra** is the cornerstone of Vaiṣṇava temple ritual and philosophy. It reveals the nature of the Supreme Lord Nārāyaṇa and the process of His manifestation into the manifest world.

### **Key Aspects**

* **Vyūha Theory** — The fourfold manifestation of the Lord as Vāsudeva, Saṅkarṣaṇa, Pradyumna, and Aniruddha.
* **Saṃhitās** — A vast corpus of over 200 texts governing ritual, meditation, and temple science.
* **Authority** — Recognized as equal to the Vedas in spiritual authority.
