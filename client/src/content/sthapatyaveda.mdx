## **Sthāpatyaveda (स्थापत्यवेद): The Science of Construction**

**Sthāpatyaveda** (also encompassing **Arthaśāstra**) is the science of manifestation in the physical and social world. It provides the geometry for temple and home construction (**Vāstu**) and the principles for ideal governance.

### **Key Aspects**

* **Vāstuśāstra** — The science of aligning physical structures with cosmic directions.
* **Arthaśāstra** — The science of political economy and righteous governance.
* **Architecture** — The art of creating spaces that reflect divine order.
