## **Sāma Veda Saṃhitā (सामवेद संहिता): The Vibration of Devotion**

The **Sāma Veda Saṃhitā** is the musical soul of the Vedas. It consists primarily of mantras from the Ṛg Veda that have been rearranged and set to celestial melodies (**Sāmans**). When chanted by the Udgātṛ priests, these melodies create a specific cosmic vibration that harmonizes the environment and the consciousness of the participants.

### **Structure**

* **Archika** — The collection of verses.
* **Gāna** — The collection of musical chants.
