## **Interpretation & Epistemology**

* **Pramāṇa-vicāraḥ (ID #185)**: The study of the six means of valid knowledge, with **Śabda** (Vedic Testimony) as the supreme authority.
* **Vidhi-vicāraḥ (ID #187)**: The "Injunction Theory" that classifies Vedic commands into six operational types (Utpatti, Viniyoga, etc.).
* **Adhikaraṇa-paddhatiḥ (ID #189)**: The standardized 5-step method of inquiry (Viṣaya, Saṃśaya, Pūrvapakṣa, Uttarapakṣa, Siddhānta).
