## **Chandas (छन्दः): The Feet of the Veda**

**Chandas** is the science of poetic meter. It provides the rhythmic foundation for the Vedic mantras, allowing them to flow with celestial harmony and mathematical precision.

### **The Primary Vedic Meters**

* **Gāyatrī** — 24 syllables.
* **Uṣṇik** — 28 syllables.
* **Anuṣṭup** — 32 syllables.
* **Bṛhatī** — 36 syllables.
* **Paṅkti** — 40 syllables.
* **Triṣṭup** — 44 syllables.
* **Jagatī** — 48 syllables.
