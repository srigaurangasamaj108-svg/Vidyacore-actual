## **Śukla Yajur Veda Brāhmaṇa (शुक्ल यजुर्वेद ब्राह्मणम्): The Śatapatha**

The Brāhmaṇa of the Śukla Yajur Veda is the famous **Śatapatha Brāhmaṇa** ("The Brāhmaṇa of a Hundred Paths"). It is the most comprehensive work on Vedic ritual, social duty, and early philosophical inquiry, providing a massive bridge between ritual action and metaphysical realization.

### **Key Characteristics**

* **Detail**: Exhaustive instructions for the Agnicayana (altar construction) and other major rituals.
* **Symbolism**: Rich in symbolic narratives that explain the cosmic significance of every ritual act.
