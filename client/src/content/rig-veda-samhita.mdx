## **Ṛg Veda Saṃhitā (ऋग्वेद संहिता): The Sound of Truth**

The **Ṛg Veda Saṃhitā** is the heart of the Vedic revelation. It consists of mantras (**Ṛks**) that were heard by the ancient Ṛṣis in their states of deep meditation. These hymns are primarily addressed to the various devas, expressing the profound relationship between the human soul and the cosmic powers.

### **Structure**

* **10 Maṇḍalas** — Large books or circles of hymns.
* **1,028 Sūktas** — Individual hymns.
* **10,552 Mantras** — The total count of sacred utterances.
