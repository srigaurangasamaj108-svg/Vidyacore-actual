## **Sāma Veda (सामवेदः): The Celestial Song**

The **Sāma Veda** consists primarily of mantras from the Ṛg Veda that have been set to specific melodies (**Sāmans**). It represents the musical and devotional aspect of Vedic revelation, emphasizing the power of sound to elevate the consciousness.

### **Key Characteristics**

* **Primary Content**: Melodic chants and songs.
* **Focus**: The ecstatic and transformative power of sacred music.
* **Priest**: Associated with the **Udgātṛ** priest.
