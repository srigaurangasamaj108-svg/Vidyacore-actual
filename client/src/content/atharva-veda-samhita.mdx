## **Atharva Veda Saṃhitā (अथर्ववेद संहिता): The Wisdom of Living**

The **Atharva Veda Saṃhitā** is the collection of sacred utterances associated with the sages Atharvan and Aṅgiras. Unlike the other three Vedas, it focuses heavily on the practicalities of human existence—offering prayers for health, longevity, protection against negative forces, and the harmony of society and the household.

### **Key Characteristics**

* **Focus**: Practical application of spiritual power.
* **Content**: Verses for healing (**Bhaiṣajya**), protection, and cosmic alignment.
* **Importance**: The source of many later sciences, including Ayurveda.
