## **Alternative Schools (अन्यसम्प्रदायाः): The Diversity of Tradition**

While the Paninian system is supreme, the Vedic tradition acknowledges other great schools of grammar that have shaped the language over millennia:

* **Aindra School**: Associated with Lord Indra, said to be the first grammar.
* **Kātantra School**: A simplified grammar for non-scholars.
* **Cāndra School**: A Buddhist tradition of Sanskrit grammar.
* **Sārasvata School**: A popular and simplified tradition dedicated to Goddess Sarasvatī.
