## **Mīmāṃsā (मीमांसा): The Science of Inquiry**

**Mīmāṃsā** is the science of critical investigation. It provides the logical tools required to understand the Vedic injunctions and reach the correct philosophical conclusions (**Siddhānta**).

### **The Two Streams of Mīmāṃsā**

1. **Pūrva Mīmāṃsā** (Jaimini) — Focusing on the ritualistic portion (**Karma-kāṇḍa**) and the philosophy of action.
2. **Uttara Mīmāṃsā** (Vyāsa) — Also known as **Vedānta**, focusing on the knowledge portion (**Jñāna-kāṇḍa**) and the nature of the Absolute.
