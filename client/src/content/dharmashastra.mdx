## **Dharmaśāstra (धर्मशास्त्रम्): The Laws of Righteousness**

**Dharmaśāstra** provides the practical guidelines for ethical living and social harmony. It outlines the duties (**Dharma**) of individuals at various stages of life (**Āśrama**) and within their social roles (**Varṇa**).

### **Key Aspects**

* **Sadācāra** (Good Conduct) — The observation of traditional ethical norms.
* **Saṃskāra** (Rituals of Purification) — The life-cycle events that refine the consciousness.
* **Vyavahāra** (Civil Law) — The rules for social interaction, justice, and governance.
