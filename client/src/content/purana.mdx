## **Purāṇa (पुराणम्): The Expansive Narrative**

The **Purāṇas** are the "fifth Veda," provided by Sage Vyāsadeva to make the deep truths of the Vedas accessible through history, allegory, and narrative. They contain the chronicles of the universe's creation, maintenance, and dissolution.

### **The Five Characteristics (Pañca-lakṣaṇa)**

1. **Sarga** — Primary creation.
2. **Pratisarga** — Secondary creation/dissolution.
3. **Vaṃśa** — Genealogies of devas and sages.
4. **Manvantara** — The periods of the various Manus.
5. **Vaṃśānucarita** — The histories of the great dynasties.
