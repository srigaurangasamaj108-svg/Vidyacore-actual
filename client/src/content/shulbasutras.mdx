## **Śulbasūtras (शुल्बसूत्राणि): The Sacred Measurement**

The **Śulbasūtras** are the "Rules of the Cord." They represent the mathematical and geometric genius of the Vedic tradition, providing the exact calculations required to construct complex altars for the Śrauta sacrifices.

### **Focus & Key Texts**

* **Focus**: Altar construction, early geometry (Pythagorean theorem roots), and mathematics.
* **Key Texts**: Baudhāyana, Āpastamba, and Kātyāyana Śulbasūtras.
