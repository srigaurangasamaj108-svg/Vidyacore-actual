## **Mishrāgama (मिश्रागम): The Path of Refining**

**Mishrāgama** (the "Mixed Path") serves as a vital bridge between external devotion and internal realization. It balances the performance of rituals and pūjā with the practice of inner yoga and meditation.

### **Foundational Principles**

* **Ontology**: A blend of ritual and philosophy, prioritizing the purification of the mind and body.
* **Practice**: Uses Mantras, Yantras, and specific pūjā methods to refine the guṇas toward Sattva.
* **Key Texts**: The **Yāmalas** (such as Rudra-Yāmala), which emphasize the dialogue between Śiva and Śakti.
