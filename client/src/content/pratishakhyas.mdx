## **Ṛgveda Prātiśākhyam (ऋग्वेदप्रातिशाख्यम्)**
Attributed to Sage Śaunaka, this text is the oldest Prātiśākhya and provides the foundational rules for the pronunciation of the Ṛgvedic hymns.

## **Yajurveda Prātiśākhyam (यजुर्वेदप्रातिशाख्यम्)**
These texts provide the phonetic rules for the Śukla (Vājasaneyi) and Kṛṣṇa (Taittirīya) streams of the Yajurveda.

## **Sāmaveda Prātiśākhyam (सामवेदप्रातिशाख्यम्)**
Focused on the melodic pronunciation and the relationship between mantra and chant.

Associated with the Śaunaka recension, detailing the unique phonetics of the fourth Veda.
