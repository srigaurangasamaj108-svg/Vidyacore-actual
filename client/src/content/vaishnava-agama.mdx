## **Vaiṣṇava Āgama (वैष्णवागम): The Path of Divine Service**

The **Vaiṣṇava Āgamas** are the foundational scriptures for the worship and service of Lord Viṣṇu and His incarnations. They provide the practical "technology" of devotion—temple construction, deity installation, and personal ritual.

### **The Two Major Streams**

1. **Pāñcarātra** — The tradition of "Five Nights," emphasized by Lord Kṛṣṇa Himself.
2. **Vaikhānasa** — The tradition of the sage Vikhānas, closely linked to the Vedic ritual.

---
