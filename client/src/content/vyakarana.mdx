## **Vyākaraṇa (व्याकरणम्): The Mouth of the Veda**

**Vyākaraṇa** is the analytical science of language. It ensures that the Sanskrit of the Vedas remains pure and unchanging, allowing the exact meaning of the mantras to be preserved across millennia.

### **The Pāṇinian Tradition**

The most authoritative work on Vyākaraṇa is **Pāṇini's Aṣṭādhyāyī**, which provides a perfect mathematical structure for the Sanskrit language.

* **Prakṛti** (Root)
* **Pratyaya** (Suffix)
* **Sandhi** (Euphoric Combination)
