## **Sāma Veda Upaniṣad (सामवेद उपनिषद्): The Song of the Absolute**

The **Upaniṣads** of the Sāma Veda contain some of the most profound and expansive realizations in the entire Vedic corpus. They focus on the identity between the individual soul and the universal reality, often using the analogy of musical harmony.

### **Primary Texts**

* **Chāndogya Upaniṣad** — One of the largest and most important, containing the Mahāvākya: **Tat Tvam Asi** ("You are That").
* **Kena Upaniṣad** — Exploring the ultimate power behind the senses and the mind.

---

> "You are that eternal Truth which sustains the universe."
