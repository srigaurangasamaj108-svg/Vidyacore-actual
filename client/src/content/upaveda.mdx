## **Upaveda (उपवेद): Wisdom in Action**

The **Upavedas** represent the practical application of Vedic principles to the various facets of human life. While the Vedas provide the spiritual foundation, the Upavedas offer the technical and practical knowledge required for excellence in material and social existence.

### **The Four Primary Upavedas**

1. **Āyurveda** (Medicine) — The science of life, health, and longevity (linked to Atharva Veda).
2. **Dhanurveda** (Martial Arts) — The science of defense and social protection (linked to Yajur Veda).
3. **Gāndharvaveda** (Music/Arts) — The science of sound, rhythm, and aesthetics (linked to Sāma Veda).
4. **Arthaśāstra** (Politics/Economics) — The science of governance and societal prosperity (linked to Atharva Veda).
