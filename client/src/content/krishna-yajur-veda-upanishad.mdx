## **Kṛṣṇa Yajur Veda Upaniṣad (कृष्ण यजुर्वेद उपनिषद्): The Realization of Bliss**

The Upaniṣads of the Kṛṣṇa Yajur Veda are cornerstone texts of Vedānta:

1. **Taittirīya Upaniṣad** — Exploring the five sheaths (**Kośas**) of the soul and the nature of Brahman as **Ānanda** (Bliss).
2. **Kaṭha Upaniṣad** — The famous dialogue between Naciketas and Lord Yama concerning the secrets of life and death.

---

> "Brahman is Truth, Knowledge, and Infinite." — **Taittirīya Upaniṣad 2.1.1**
